Stars

June 9, 2014 thedaniel Tweet

We’ve added stars to Atom.io. Stars are a new way to keep track of packages you find interesting. You can see the packages you’ve starred on your user page, and also explore what other users have starred.

Stars are supported by apm as well. You can list your stars, and star and unstar packages - check out apm help stars for the full docs.

Stars can also be used to set up a new install of Atom with your favorite packages - just run apm stars --install, and everything you’ve starred will be installed. You can also try out other users’ Atom setups by installing their stars:

apm stars --user thedaniel --install
Installing autocomplete-emojis to /Users/daniel/.atom/packages ✓
Installing block-travel to /Users/daniel/.atom/packages ✓
Installing color-picker to /Users/daniel/.atom/packages ✓
Installing go-plus to /Users/daniel/.atom/packages ✓
Installing linter to /Users/daniel/.atom/packages ✓
Installing pomodoro to /Users/daniel/.atom/packages ✓

We’re looking forward to seeing what packages and themes you star!
